Aqui geralmente fica contato

Busey Ipsum was designed to alleviate a void in the placeholder text generation market that only one man's direct quotes
could fill: Gary Busey. His enligtening knowledge of the world is so gracefully put into anagrams and quick sound bites,
all while holding no preconcieved notions of anything, especially gender. Sometimes his knowledge is so great that we don't
even know what he means. Sometimes we wish we had a Busey translator, but that is not the point of this website.
It is meant to jumble the beauty of the Busey into neatly packed paragraphs for use in laying out your next website,
pamphlet, television commercial or business card.
